Tower Smasher

How to play the game:

1.) Use the ball wrecker by pressing your mouse and release

at an appropriate position to lauch it.

2.)Knock out the boxes.Each box is 20 points.

3.)Press "SPACEBAR" to reset ball wrecker's position

4.)Each time you press "SPACEBAR" , Reset Turns increases

By 1. At 6 reset Turns , if score is'nt 360 , you lose.

5.)Press R to reset the Score and Reset Turns

6.)Knock out all boxes to win
